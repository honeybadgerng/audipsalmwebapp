SAP development and consulting services
for full-scale business transformation What we offer

SAP custom development

We provide full-cycle SAP development services and support to deliver high-quality solutions, which include ABAP programming, code optimization, Fiori, custom SAPUI5 development, integration technologies using PI/PO, REST, SOAP services, etc.

SAP consulting services

We conduct an overview and analysis of the existing SAP landscape and provide L2-L3 resources, including customization and, if required, SAP Custom Development services. The responsible team implements change requests in your SAP environment, considering specifics you already have and its SAP-related integrations.

SAP OpenText integration

With over 15 years of experience in OpenText consulting, we know how to integrate your SAP product with the OpenText suite. Whether you seek to track engineering documents or automate data entry, streamline collaboration or ensure accurate reporting, the OpenText and SAP services we provide cover all these areas.

SAP Application Management (AMS)

We help you optimize resources and minimize risks for using your SAP Environment within L1-L2 support resolving functional and technical (Basis, ABAP) issues.

SAP BI/BO, BW development

N-iX team, as a professional SAP service provider, knows how to develop, implement, and maintain Business Objects, Business Intelligence, and Business Warehouse solutions.

SAP Rollouts

Have a lack of internal resources for the rollout of a corporate SAP Global Template used in headquarters? We may help with fit/gap analysis, local requirements analysis, and implementation of specific business requirements you have.

SAP Cloud services

N-iX SAP IT consulting leverages the SAP cloud's capabilities to exploit their SAP products' full potential while cutting infrastructure costs. Migration of your SAP to AWS, Azure, and GCP is in focus here. SAP BTP Analytics Cloud and Cloud Development for SAP are also in our scope!

SAP Hybris integration

N-iX consultants deliver cutting-edge B2B and B2C e-commerce solutions by integrating Hybris with SAP products. We can incorporate diverse Hybris APIs into the SAP ecosystem to build advanced and scalable e-commerce systems.
