N-iX DevOps implementation lifecycle

01. Perform infrastructure evaluation

  • Evaluate the DevOps maturity of your infrastructure;
  • Acquire a detailed roadmap for implementing your DevOps strategy;
  • Obtain technical recommendations for the most efficient and cost-effective development process.

02. Establish a DevOps Center of Excellence

  • Accumulate, document, and share critical knowledge;
  • Define and standardize the techniques and best practices used across your organization;
  • Ensure quicker business innovation by improving the performance of your DevOps teams.

03. Set clear goals and KPIs

  • Set tangible goals that you aim to achieve with DevOps transformation;
  • Streamline communication, define roles and responsibilities, and share collaboration awareness between your teams;
  • Establish an effective DevOps culture that makes meeting goals easier.

04. Set up project in-take

  • Acquire the most optimal tools and development methods;
  • Boost the speed and efficiency of DevOps implementation services;
  • Gather constant feedback from your teams to improve project intake processes.

05. Run and analyze pilot programs

  • Identify a process that can be used as a model for enterprise level scaling;
  • Apply your DevOps tools and methodologies to the chosen process;
  • Analyze the results and gather feedback from the teams involved in the program.

06. Scale your DevOps processes

  • Apply DevOps processes to the entire delivery pipeline;
  • Analyze and compare new KPIs with the existing ones;
  • Continuously gather feedback and introduce improvements to make development even faster and more efficient.
