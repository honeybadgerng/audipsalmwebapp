Our story

N-iX started out as Novellix — a product startup founded by Andrew Pavliv (N-iX CEO), Dmytro Kosarev, and Werner Richard Kreiner back in 2002. Novellix was developing Novell’s product applications for the Linux platform. The company got attention from Novell’s top management, so Novell acquired the technology from Novellix and became the first big customer of re-branded N-iX, a software development service company.

Since then, N-iX has built a strong presence across Europe and the Americas, helping world’s leading organizations turn challenges into lasting business value, operational efficiency, and revenue growth using advanced technology. Being in business for over two decades, we have worked with dozens of tech companies, Fortune 500 leaders, and enterprises of all sizes, creating value across a wide variety of sectors, including finance, manufacturing, supply chain, retail, e-commerce, healthcare, and more.

N-iX Co-founders Dmytro Kosarev (left) and Andrew Pavliv (right) [Lviv, 2003]

Over the past few years, N-iX has made technology maturity a strategic priority, investing in advanced technological expertise, project management practices, and ecosystem partnerships. We have greatly expanded our Technology Office - a consulting unit that provides expertise in Architecture, Cloud, Big Data, AI/ML, IoT, UI/UX, Digital Platforms, and Cybersecurity. Moreover, we are continuously growing our tech ecosystem and partnerships with AWS, GCP, Microsoft, SAP, OpenText, Snowflake, and other tech leaders shaping the future of industries across the globe.

N-iX is recognized by numerous industry awards, such as CRN Solution Provider 500, Global Outsourcing 100 by IAOP, ISG Provider Lens™, Modern Application Development services providers by Forrester, to name a few. Also, our company has always been actively involved in local communities supporting charities, events, NGOs, and numerous initiatives, as we pursue our mission to make a positive impact on the lives of our clients, our people, and our society by leveraging technology and innovation.

Company values

Empathy
We believe that empathy is essential for the success of our company, our clients, and every single person at N-iX. We care about the work we do and its impact. We care about our clients and our teammates. N-iXers are curious and always willing to look at the situation from a different perspective. This is what drives high commitment and strong collaboration at N-iX.
Openness
Innovation and growth are impossible without open communication, honesty, and trust. At N-iX, we cut out any signs of bureaucracy, hidden agendas, and favoritism. Instead, we are always ready to share information, we are open to feedback, and have clear communication channels. This allows us to make informed decisions and take ownership of them.
Flexibility
We want every person at N-iX to feel empowered and free to take the initiative. So we create an environment where it’s ok to make mistakes, learn from them, improve, and grow. We are ardent advocates of Agile methodologies, and try to use Agile across all our processes and projects.
Entrepreneurship
Entrepreneurship is all about innovation, responsibility, and a sense of ownership. At N-iX, we value initiative and use a get-things-done approach in our work. N-iXers treat every project as their own small enterprise, and this helps us deliver maximum value to our clients. By closely following market trends, we often become the first on the market to develop new expertise.

Our timeline

2002 2003 2004 2005 2006 2007 2008 2009 2010 2011 2012 2013 2014 2015 2016 2017 2018 2019 2020 2021 2022

3 professionals

Three partners started Novellix (later N-iX) in an attempt to create software for Linux that would allow to work with Novell NetWare® and GroupWise®. They named the company Novellix, combining words Novell and Linux.

6 professionals

Novellix (later N-iX) fully developed the software and made the prototype available for downloading within half a year. This was noticed by the management at Novell, who had long ago seen the trend and had been developing a strategy to approach Linux. Novell decided to acquire the technology and to develop it further with the development team at Novellix (later renamed to N-iX at Novell’s request). Therefore, Novell becomes the first company to partner with N-iX according the Dedicated Development Team/Center mode

12 professionals

First positive references appear and N-iX starts cooperation with new Customers to develop high-tech engineering solutions. N-iX launches Products Development Laboratory to research and produce solutions together with improvements for business processes. One of the initial products is a CD running N-iX Linux with connection to NetWare service.

25 professionals

By the reference from VP of Novell N-iX starts collaboration with StreamServe (now part of OpenText), a Swedish document presenter provider, and launches the long-term Dedicated Development Centre. This event shapes the whole future for N-iX , changing the focus of the company from simple software development to the model of Dedicated Development Centers. This transformation leads to an impressive list of short-term software and hardware projects accomplished. N-iX enters into the financial domain with MyForex Application by Finatek Dedicated Development Centre which allows nearly real-time processing and handling hundreds of messages.

30 professionals

New services such as Professional Services, QA and Maintenance are added into the R&D team of StreamServe at N-iX. Dynamic development of cooperation with StreamServe drives N-iX to launch a brand new service, Professional Services Crew for StreamServe. N-iX starts implementation of the Scandinavian business standards and approaches towards business management. This decision helps N-iX to grow with 2 new Scandinavian Clients.

40 professionals

Culture of innovations that is constantly cultivated at N-iX brings new interesting high-tech nearshore project from Mediornet an Austrian hardware engineering company. N-iX manages to finalize the delivery of full-cycle development services and starts Support Engineering Service of 2nd and 3rd lines with StreamServe. N-iX initiates transformation of the business model into the Cost+ to provide customers with easy and transparent explanations of the value and cost of their projects.

50 professionals

N-iX focuses on the existing Clients. Big attention is devoted to the quality of Services that were integrated during the last two years. N-iX makes a strong accent on management competencies. As a result, in one year N-iX becomes a Microsoft Certified Partner and SUN Associated Partner for its outstanding performance. High-tech focus brings N-iX new projects that require competence in the most progressive technologies both in hardware and software architecture. Therefore, N-iX is actively developing its engineering expertise to deliver cutting-edge solutions to its new Clients. N-iX invests in R&D growth and this enables the company to gain competencies in high-tech services delivery.

60 professionals

N-iX enters a brand new market of house controlling systems (a.k.a. smart house systems). The first 6 months pass very intensively: new office in Sweden, participation in CEBIT and various expositions demonstrating company expertise. N-iX enters deeply into the financial domain through the development of services and extension of cooperation with stock-exchange projects.

80 professionals

N-iX starts collaboration with innovative projects and invests into startups from the USA and Europe. An innovative application for mobile devices ThickButtons comes on the stage. Besides startups, N-iX launches the Professional Services Team for implementation of design for complete architecture of complex systems – Genpact. This client helps to define the business-model for own business operations based on the “Cost+” approach.

130 professionals

The largest Canadian software development company, OpenText, becomes an N-iX customer. Having mobile expertise, N-iX is selected by OpenText Mobile to run outsourcing services for its new acquisition breakthrough technology, Mobile Wave Platform. Along with big Clients services start to grow and projects evolve. The products complexity increases a possibility of inaccuracies and bugs. In order to maintain the high quality of services, N-iX opens a Test House. The Test House consolidates all the projects’ test teams under the same standards and methods to maintain consistency and structure in N-iX products. Apart from core activities, N-iX focuses on innovations and this passion leads the company to new experience with startups. As a result, this year brings 2 new startup Clients: Recorded Future and Meshin, a Xerox Funded Startup at Palo Alto Research Centre.

140 professionals

N-iX celebrates its 10 year anniversary. DDC model proves its efficiency and exceeds expectations since major Dedicated Development Teams in N-iX history keep their growth. Due to constant development of projects N-iX team grows to more than 140 professionals. In the upcoming year, N-iX anticipates an increase in teams and the rise of new interesting projects to gain new experience and expertise.

150 professionals

N-iX started collaboration with Currency Cloud, a UK financial technology company that owns and operates an international payments technology platform. The platform delivers FX conversion and payments as a cloud based web service and allows its customers to deliver any foreign exchange payments related service to one another simpler and at a lower cost than regular service providers. N-iX partnered with SCHAD, the owner of a technology that integrates IBM knowledge management system and meter data to monitor and control various processes from a cell phone. N-iX set up a team of developers for a US technology media startup that is building a powerful, cutting-edge platform that instantly connects people everywhere with thousands of professional journalists and original news content providers, fostering quality journalism and enabling faster, more effective decisions. N-iX started collaboration with Book2Meet, a Belgian company that makes meeting rooms bookable online.

170 professionals

N-iX developed and released an online booking platform together with and for Book2Meet. The platform allows users to find a meeting space nearby whether they are looking for a business center, a meeting room in a hotel, or the hidden gem of the city. N-iX started collaboration with 8 new companies that work in industries ranging from Financial Services and Communications to Document Processing. The companies include Orbus Software, Kodime,Top Connect, Debreuck Neirynck, b-to-v, Agilis, Addoro and Spamdrain. Client geography includes the UK, Belgium, Switzerland, Germany, Sweden and the Baltics.
