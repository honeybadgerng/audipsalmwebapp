Your digital transformation step by step

1. Business transformation readiness assessment

Together with key stakeholders, we thoroughly analyze the maturity level of your organization and create the business case, define the goals and objectives for the target business vision, as well as assess the readiness of your business for this transformation.

2. Strategic deep dive

Next, we create a definitive roadmap, define clear KPIs, and identify success metrics that help set a course for successful transformation.

3. Technology consulting

Once we have a full picture of the desired state and key objectives, we define the best solution to help you reach your goals and determine the necessary tech stack.

4. Discovery Phase

During the Discovery Phase, we'll finalize the scope of your solution. This process is based on business readiness assessment, coordination of business stakeholders, the user journey map, market research, and other key aspects. It provides all the deliverables needed to start implementation efficiently.

5. Implementation

We develop the needed solutions and implement operational changes needed to get you to the desired state. The N-iX expert takes control over the implementation process, taking care of software engineering, project management, and quality assurance through regular assessment, while tracking the success of the process.

6. Scaling and quality assurance

After successful completion of the previous stage, our experts conduct rigorous testing and employ top quality assurance protocols to ensure that the final solution is up to the best standards. We also help you scale the transformation throughout your entire organization.

7. Handover and knowledge transfer

When the solution is ready or transformation is over, we conduct a thorough knowledge transfer, handing over available technical documentation: architecture overview, release notes, presentations, and other. We also conduct webinars and video conferences for key stakeholders when needed.
